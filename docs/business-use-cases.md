# Business Use Cases

Oil Intelligence AI is intended for teams that need fast, explainable context across physical oil markets, geopolitics, maritime logistics, and operational risk.

## Target Users

- Oil traders and commodity desks.
- Logistics companies and maritime intelligence teams.
- Fuel distributors and downstream planners.
- Mining companies and industrial fuel buyers.
- Agribusiness teams exposed to diesel and freight costs.
- Banks, research teams, and commodity analysts.
- Energy companies with upstream, midstream, or refining exposure.

## Example Workflows

- Morning crude and product market intelligence brief.
- Monitoring chokepoint disruption risk for shipping decisions.
- Tracking refinery outage impact on diesel and gasoline cracks.
- Watching inventory drawdowns and production changes.
- Preparing scenario analysis for geopolitical events.
- Screening satellite/fire/storage signals for operational alerts.

## Disclaimer

The platform is an intelligence and workflow prototype. It does not provide financial, trading, investment, legal, or operational safety advice.
